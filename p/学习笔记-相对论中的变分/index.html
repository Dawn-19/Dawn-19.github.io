广义相对论中的变分

  1. 爱因斯坦场方程

    在广义相对论的框架下，考虑物质与引力的耦合，忽略宇宙学常数，可以给出如下作用量:

    $$ S = \int \mathrm{d}^4x \sqrt{-g} \left( \frac{M_{\text{P}}^2}{2}R+\mathscr{L}_m \right) ~, $$

    其中$g_{\mu\nu}$为对应的时空度规，这里取号差为$(-,+,+,+)$，$M_\text{P}^2 = 1/8\pi G$为普朗克质量，$R = g_{\mu\nu}R^{\mu\nu}$为里奇标量，$R_{\mu\nu} = R_{ ~ \mu\lambda\nu}^{\lambda}$为里奇张量，$R_{~ \mu\lambda\nu}^{\sigma}$为黎曼张量，$\mathscr{L}_m$为拉氏量，其能动张量定义为

    $$ T_{\mu\nu}^{(m)} = -\frac{2}{\sqrt{-g}}\frac{\delta }{\delta g^{\mu\nu}} (\sqrt{-g} \mathscr{L}_m) =-2\frac{\partial \mathscr{L}_m}{\partial g^{\mu\nu}} +g_{\mu\nu} \mathscr{L}_m ~. $$

    这里$\frac{1}{\sqrt{-g}}\frac{\delta }{\delta g^{\mu\nu}} \sqrt{-g} = -\frac{1}{2}g_{\mu\nu}$ . 对度规场作变分，引力部分有

    $$ \begin{split} \delta \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g}\frac{M_\text{P}^2}{2}R &= \frac{M_{\text{P}}^2}{2} \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \left( R\delta\sqrt{-g}+\sqrt{-g}R \right) \\ & = \frac{M_{\text{P}}^2}{2} \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \left[ -\frac{1}{2}\frac{1}{\sqrt{-g}}R\delta g +\sqrt{-g}\left(R_{\mu\nu}\delta g^{\mu\nu} + g^{\mu\nu}\delta R_{\mu\nu} \right) \right]\\ & = \frac{M_{\text{P}}^2}{2} \int \mathrm{d}^4 x\sqrt{-g}\left[ \left( -\frac{1}{2}g_{\mu\nu}R+ R_{\mu\nu} \right)\delta g^{\mu\nu} + \nabla_{\nu} \left( g^{\mu\nu}\delta\Gamma_{\lambda\mu}^{\nu}-g^{\mu\nu}\delta\Gamma_{\lambda\mu}^\lambda \right)\right]\\ \end{split} $$

    其中$\delta g = -g g_{\mu\nu}\delta g^{\mu\nu}$，协变导数全微分积分也是边界项，结果为0（这里似乎有争议，但先暂且这么认为吧）. 记$G_{\mu\nu} = R_{\mu\nu}-\frac{1}{2}g_{\mu\nu}R$ 为爱因斯坦张量，对物质场作变分可得

    $$ \begin{split} \delta \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g}\mathscr{L}_m &= \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \left( \sqrt{-g}\delta \mathscr{L}_m + \mathscr{L}_m\delta\sqrt{-g} \right)\\ &=\int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g}\left( \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}_m}{\partial g^{\mu\nu}}-\frac{1}{2} g_{\mu\nu}\mathscr{L}_m \right)\delta g^{\mu\nu} \\ & =- \frac{1}{2}\int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g} T_{\mu\nu}~, \end{split} $$

    最终可以得到爱因斯坦场方程：

    $$ G_{\mu\nu} = 8\pi G T_{\mu\nu}. $$

  2. Klein-Gorden方程

    在极早期宇宙学中，暴胀可以认为是由一种标量场——暴胀场驱动的，其作用量为1

    $$ S = \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g} [ -\frac{1}{2}g^{\mu\nu}\partial_\mu \phi \partial_\nu \phi - V(\phi) ] $$

    其中$V(\phi)$为暴胀场的potential，具体形式与选择的模型有关，对其变分有

    $$ \begin{aligned} \delta S &= \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g} [-\frac{1}{2}g^{\mu\nu}\partial_\mu \phi \partial_\nu \delta \phi -\frac{1}{2}g^{\mu\nu}\partial_\mu \delta \phi \partial_\nu \phi - \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}\phi}V(\phi)\delta\phi] \\ &=\int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g}[-\nabla_\mu \phi \nabla^\mu \delta \phi - \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}\phi}V(\phi)\delta\phi ]\\ &= \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g} [-\nabla^\mu(\nabla_\mu\phi\delta\phi) + (\nabla^\mu \nabla_\mu \phi - \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}\phi}V(\phi))\delta\phi ]\\ \end{aligned} $$

    消去边界项，得到Klein-Gorden方程

    $$ \Box \phi = V'(\phi) \Leftrightarrow \frac{1}{\sqrt{-g}}\partial_\mu (\sqrt{-g} g^{\mu\nu} \partial_\nu \phi) = V'(\phi) $$

    其中$V^\prime (\phi) = \mathrm{d}V/\mathrm{d}\phi$，注意在广义相对论中有关系$\nabla_\mu\nabla^\mu \phi = \frac{1}{\sqrt{-g}}\partial_\mu (\sqrt{-g} g^{\mu\nu} \partial_\nu \phi)$，可见.

    此外，用能动量守恒能得到相同的结果，暴胀场的能动张量为：

    $$ \begin{aligned} T_{\mu\nu} &= -2 \frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial g^{\mu\nu}} + g_{\mu\nu}\mathscr{L}\\ &= \nabla_\mu\phi\nabla_\nu \phi - \frac{1}{2}g_{\mu\nu}g^{\alpha\beta} \nabla_\alpha \phi \nabla_\beta \phi - g_{\mu\nu}V(\phi)~. \end{aligned} $$

    由标量场能动张量守恒，可得

    $$ \begin{split} \nabla^{\mu}T_{\mu\nu} &=-g_{\mu\nu}g^{\alpha\beta}(\nabla^\mu \nabla_\alpha \phi)\nabla_\beta \phi - g_{\mu\nu} V'(\phi) \nabla^\mu \phi +(\nabla^\mu \nabla_\mu)\nabla_\nu \phi + \nabla_\mu \phi (\nabla^\mu \nabla_\nu \phi )\\ &=-(\nabla_\nu \nabla_\alpha \phi)\nabla^\alpha \phi - V'(\phi)\nabla_\nu \phi +(\nabla^\mu\nabla_\mu)\nabla_\nu \phi + \nabla^\alpha \phi (\nabla_\alpha \nabla_\nu \phi)\\ &=\nabla^\alpha \phi [\nabla_\alpha,\nabla_\nu]\phi + (\nabla^\mu \nabla_\mu \phi - V'(\phi))\nabla_\nu \phi=0\,,\\ &\Rightarrow \nabla^\mu \nabla_\mu \phi - V'(\phi)=0 \,. \end{split} $$

  3. 四维真空Maxwell方程

    真空中电磁场的作用量为

    $$ S = -\frac{1}{4} \int \mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g}F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu}~. $$

    其中$F_{\mu\nu} = \nabla_\mu A_\nu - \nabla_\nu A_\mu $为电磁场张量，$A^\mu = (\varphi,\vec{A})$为四维电矢势，对作用量进行变分：

    $$ \begin{aligned} \delta S & = -\frac{1}{4}\delta\int \mathrm{d}^4x\sqrt{-g} F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu}\\ & = -\frac{1}{2}\int\mathrm{d}^4x \sqrt{-g}\delta F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu}\\ & = -\frac{1}{2} \int\mathrm{d}^4x\sqrt{-g} F^{\mu\nu} \delta(\nabla_\mu A_\nu -\nabla_\nu A_\mu)\\ & = -\frac{1}{2} \int\mathrm{d}^4x\sqrt{-g} F^{\mu\nu} \nabla_\mu\delta A_\nu -\frac{1}{2} \int\mathrm{d}^4x\sqrt{-g} (-F^{\nu\mu})(- \nabla_\nu\delta A_\mu)\\ & = -\int\mathrm{d}^4 x\sqrt{-g} F^{\mu\nu} \nabla_\mu \delta A_\nu\\ & = \int\mathrm{d}^4 x \sqrt{-g}[- \nabla_\mu (F^{\mu\nu} \delta A_\nu) + \nabla_\mu F^{\mu\nu}\delta A_\nu]~, \end{aligned} $$

    这里利用了电磁场张量的反对称性$F_{\mu\nu} = -F_{\nu\mu}$，将上式消去边界项，即有$\nabla_\mu F^{\mu\nu} = 0$. 此外，由Bianchi恒等式可知$\nabla_{(\mu}F_{\nu\sigma)} = 0$，所以真空中四维形式的Maxwell方程组为

    $$ \nabla_\mu F^{\mu\nu} = 0 ~ ,\quad \nabla_{(\mu}F_{\nu\sigma)} = 0~. $$

    真空中电磁场的能动张量：

    $$ \begin{aligned} T_{\mu\nu} &= -\frac{2}{\sqrt{-g}} \frac{\delta}{\delta g^{\mu\nu}} \left( -\frac{1}{4} \sqrt{-g} F_{\alpha\beta} F^{\alpha\beta} \right), \\ &= -\frac{2}{\sqrt{-g}} \left\{ \frac{\delta \sqrt{-g}}{\delta g^{\mu\nu}} (-\frac{1}{4} F_{\alpha\beta} F^{\alpha\beta}) + \sqrt{-g} \frac{\delta}{\delta g^{\mu\nu}} \left( -\frac{1}{4} F_{\alpha\beta} F_{\lambda\sigma} g^{\lambda\alpha} g^{\sigma\beta} \right) \right\} \\ &= -\frac{1}{4} g_{\mu\nu} F_{\alpha\beta} F^{\alpha\beta} - 2 \left( -\frac{1}{4} \right) \left[ F_{\alpha\beta} F_{\lambda\sigma} (\delta^{\lambda}_{~\mu} \delta^{\alpha}_{~\nu}g^{\sigma\beta}+ \delta^\sigma_{~\mu} \delta^\beta_{~\nu}g^{\lambda\alpha} \right] \\ &= -\frac{1}{4} g_{\mu\nu} F_{\alpha\beta} F^{\alpha\beta} + \frac{1}{2} (F_{\nu}^{~ \sigma}F_{\mu\sigma} + F_{\alpha \nu}F^\alpha _{~\mu})\\ & = -\frac{1}{4} g_{\mu\nu} F_{\alpha\beta} F^{\alpha\beta} - F_\mu^{~\alpha }F_{\alpha \nu} \end{aligned} $$

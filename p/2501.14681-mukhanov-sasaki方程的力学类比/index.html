2501.14681-Mukhanov-Sasaki方程的力学类比

Mukhanov-Sasaki方程的力学类比，包括格林函数的计算。

Mar 25, 2025

Sec2.2 力学类比

广义上来说只要 $u_k ^{\prime \prime} + (k^2-\frac{z ^{\prime \prime}}{z})u_k =0 $ 中的 $ \frac{z ^{\prime \prime}}{z}$ 发生较为剧烈变化就能够在某些$k$处的功率谱振荡。

  • 简要推导：

    对于Mukhanov-Sasaki方程，设$u_k = r_k e^{i\theta_k}, r_k,\theta_k$都含共形时间$\tau$，则有

    $$ \begin{aligned} &\text{Real:} ~ r_k ^{\prime \prime} + (k^2-\frac{z ^{\prime \prime}}{z}-\theta_k'^2)r_k = 0 ~ ,\\ &\text{Imaginary:}~ 2r_k'\theta_k'+r_k'\theta_k ^{\prime \prime}= 0 ~ \Rightarrow ~ r_k^2\theta_k' = L \end{aligned} $$

    虚部方程类似于旋转系统中的角动量守恒，对于Bunch-Davies vacuum，$u_k \sim \frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}}e^{-ik\tau}$，那么

    $$ r_k = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}},\quad \theta_k' = -k,\quad L = -\frac{1}{2}~. $$

    那么初始由Bunch-Davies vacuum 演化的扰动模始终满足

    $$ \theta_k' = - \frac{1}{2r_k^2}~. $$

    取$V_k = \frac{1}{2}(k^2 - \frac{z ^{\prime \prime}}{z})r_k^2 + \frac{1}{8r_k^2} $，那么$r_k$的演化方程可以写为

    $$ r_k'' + V_{r_k}'(r_k) = 0 $$

    由于$V_k(r_k)$中$z ^{\prime \prime}/z$显含时间，那么可以认为这个（等价的）旋转系统的能量不守恒，且在$r_k \to 0$时势能发散，这表明功率谱永不消逝(角动量始终存在).

    1. 当扰动模处于 Sub-Horizon时， $k^2$ 主导演化， $z ^{\prime \prime}/z$ 是次要的影响，那么当

      • $z ^{\prime \prime}/z $缓慢变化时，$ r_k $ 绝热地趋向于$V(r_k)$的极小值点 $r_k^{min} $演化，没有明显的振荡。

      • $z ^{\prime \prime}/z$剧烈变化时，方程可以改为$r_k ^{\prime \prime} + 4k^2(r-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}}) = 0$，$r_k$会在$\frac{1}{2k}$附近振荡，也就是

        $$ r_k -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}} \propto \sin(2k(\tau-\tau_0)+\varphi_0) $$

        这里初始相位随着$k$线性增长，也就是在功率谱上有振荡的表现，周期为$-\pi/2\tau_0$.

        新方程的推导：

        $$ \begin{aligned} r_k'' + V_{r_k}'(r_k) &= 0 \Rightarrow r_k'' + V_{r_k r_k}''(\frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}})(r_k-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}})= 0 ~,\\ V_{r_k r_k}''(\frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}}) &= (k^2 - \frac{z''}{z}) + \frac{3}{4}r_k^{-4} \\ &\simeq 4k^2\\ \Rightarrow r_k'' + & 4k^2(r_k - \frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}}) = 0 \end{aligned} $$

    2. 当扰动模退出视界，也就是$k\tau \simeq -1$时，$z ^{\prime \prime}/z$在势能$V_k$中取主导作用，这会导致

      $$ r_k'' - \frac{z''}{z}r_k = 0,\quad \frac{r_k''}{r_k} = \frac{z''}{z}~. $$

      扰动模$\gamma \propto r_k/z \sim Const$.

格林函数

对于微分方程：

$$ \hat{L} u(\tau) = f(\tau)~, $$

其中 $\hat{L}$ 是微分算子，$u(\tau)$ 是待解函数，$f(\tau)$ 是已知的任意函数，设格林函数$G(\tau, \tau^\prime)$满足

$$ \hat{L}G(\tau,\tau') = \delta(\tau-\tau')~, $$

那么有

$$ \begin{aligned} &\int \hat{L}G(\tau,\tau')f(\tau')\mathrm{d}\tau' = \int \delta(\tau-\tau')f(\tau')\mathrm{d}\tau' = f(\tau)~, \\ & \Rightarrow \hat{L} \int G(\tau,\tau')f(\tau')\mathrm{d}\tau' = f(\tau)~,\\ & \Rightarrow u(\tau) = \int G(\tau,\tau')f(\tau')\mathrm{d}\tau'~. \end{aligned} $$

对于Mukhanov-Sasaki 方程，扰动模式 $k$ 在经历 Transition 后退出视界，在 Transition 阶段时仍在视界内，故$ k^2 $起主导作用，这时把 $z ^{\prime \prime}/z $ 当作小量处理：

$$ u_k''+k^2 u_k = \frac{z''}{z}u_k~. $$

方程的其次解有

$$ u_{1,\text{sub}}^{(0)} (\tau) = e^{ik\tau}~, \quad u_{2,\text{sub}}^{(0)} (\tau) = e^{-ik\tau}~. $$

用格林函数给出方程的特解

$$ u_s(\tau) = -\frac{1}{2ki}\int_{\tau_\text{I}}^\tau \mathrm{d}\tau' ~ G_{\text{sub}}(\tau,\tau')u_k(\tau')\frac{z''}{z}(\tau')~. $$

由格林函数需要满足

$$ G''(\tau,\tau')+k^2G(\tau,\tau') = \delta(\tau-\tau')~, $$

可以解出

$$ G_{\text{sub}}(\tau,\tau') = \theta(\tau-\tau') 2i \frac{\sin\left( k(\tau-\tau')\right) }{k}~. $$

  • 解的验证，根据二阶微分方程的参数变异法，对于一般的非齐次二阶微分方程

    $$ y'' + p(x)y' + Q(x) y = R(x)~, $$

    有通解（请看参数变异法）

    $$ y_p(x) = u_1(x)y_1(x)+ u_2(x)y_2(x)~. $$

    其中$y_1(x), y_2(x) $ 是二阶微分方程$y ^{\prime \prime}+p(x)y^\prime+Q(x)y = 0$ 的齐次解，$u_1, u_2$的形式如下

    $$ u_1(x) = - \int\frac{y_2}{W_{y_1 y_2}}R(x)\mathrm{d}x ~,\quad u_2(x) = \int\frac{y_1}{W_{y_1 y_2}}R(x)\mathrm{d}x~, $$

    这里$W_{y_1 y_2} = y_1 y_2^\prime -y_2 y_1^\prime $ .

    对于格林函数满足 $G ^{\prime \prime}(\tau,\tau^\prime)+k^2G(\tau,\tau^\prime) = \delta(\tau-\tau^\prime)$，其有齐次解

    $$ u_{1,\text{sub}}^{(0)} (\tau) = e^{ik\tau}~, \quad u_{2,\text{sub}}^{(0)} (\tau) = e^{-ik\tau}~. $$

    且 $W_{12} = -2ik$ ，那么对于 $G_{\text{sub}}(\tau,\tau^\prime) = G(\tau,\tau^\prime)/W_{12}$ 有

    $$ \begin{aligned} G_{\text{sub}}(\tau,\tau')&=-e^{ik\tau}\int e^{-ik\tau} \delta(\tau-\tau')\mathrm{d}\tau + e^{-ik\tau}\int e^{ik\tau} \delta(\tau-\tau')\mathrm{d}\tau\\ & = -e^{ik\tau} e^{-ik\tau'}\theta(\tau-\tau') + e^{-ik\tau} e^{ik\tau'}\theta(\tau-\tau')\\ &=\theta(\tau-\tau') \left(e^{ik(\tau'-\tau)}-e^{-ik(\tau'-\tau)} \right)\\ & = \theta(\tau-\tau')2i\sin(\tau'-\tau)~. \end{aligned} $$

参数变异法

​ 对于一般的非齐次二阶微分方程 $y ^{\prime \prime} + p(x)y^\prime + Q(x) y = R(x)$的通解$y_p$ , 其导数为：

$$ \begin{aligned} y_p' &= u_1' y_1 + u_1 y_1' + u_2' y_2 + u_2 y_2'~, \\ y_p'' & = u_1'' y_1 + 2 u_1' y_1' + u_1 y_1'' + u_2'' y_2 + 2 u_2' y_2' + u_2 y_2''~, \end{aligned} $$

​ 令 $ u_1^\prime y_1 + u_2^\prime y_2 = 0$，由于 $y_1,y_2$ 本身是齐次解，那么把 $y_p$ 代入到微分方程中,

  1. $y_p ^{\prime \prime}$中的$y_1 ^{\prime \prime}, y_2 ^{\prime \prime}$项会被消去，同时对$ u_1^\prime y_1 + u_2^\prime y_2 = 0$求导可得$u_1 ^{\prime \prime}y_1 + u_1^\prime y_1^\prime + u_2 ^{\prime \prime}y_2 + u_2^\prime y_2^\prime = 0$，最终只留下$u_1^\prime y_1^\prime +u_2^\prime y_2^\prime $.

  2. $y_p^\prime $ 由于 $ u_1^\prime y_1 + u_2^\prime y_2 = 0$ 同时 $y_1^\prime , y_2^\prime $ 被消去则没有留下来的项，

  3. $y_p $ 项中没有能够留下来的，

  4. 最终剩下 $ u_1^\prime y_1^\prime +u_2^\prime y_2^\prime = R(x)$ .

​ 需要求解的方程变为

$$ u_1' y_1 + u_2' y_2 = 0 ~, \quad u_1' y_1' + u_2' y_2' = R(x) ~. $$

​ 由此代入

$$ u_1(x) = - \int\frac{y_2}{W_{y_1 y_2}}R(x)\mathrm{d}x ~,\quad u_2(x) = \int\frac{y_1}{W_{y_1 y_2}}R(x)\mathrm{d}x~, $$

​ 发现两个方程都满足。所以最终通解为 ​

$$ y_p(x) = u_1(x)y_1(x)+ u_2(x)y_2(x)~. $$
